How to use the mining course?

Step 1

Turn on the miner and connect to the network. Each miner or storage device requires at least one Filenet token for the mining tutorial to be used after account startup.

Step 2

Conduct mining parameter configuration, please see mining tutorial for details.

Step 3

After successful configuration, you can dig filenet. See blockchain browser or fn wallet for mining revenue.
