Filenet Fog Computing

Fog computing is an extension of the concept of cloud computing. Filenet fog computing is a future-oriented fog computing technology, a paravirtualized service computing architecture model that enables billions of smart devices to conveniently share idle computing, storage, bandwidth and other resources. Filenet fog computing is not only a resource pool that spans the center to the edge of the network, but also a new point-to-point delivery system that provides PaaS platform and SaaS services. Filenet fog computing helps companies reduce content distribution costs and improve quality while helping end users get income through sharing.
